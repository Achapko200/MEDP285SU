Step into Cotton Candy Canyon, where the trees are pink, the clouds taste sweet, and everyone has a sticky smile.
Rocket Rollercoaster Ridge launches you into loops of laughter and stomach flips — hold on to your space helmet!
The Jellybean Jungle is a maze of candy-colored creatures and marshmallow vines. Don’t get stuck in the licorice!
Pirate Penguin Pier is home to waddling buccaneers who demand fish sticks and giggles in equal measure.
At Rainbow River Rapids, the water sparkles like glitter and the lifeguards wear unicorn horns for safety.
Enter Robo-Ranch, where robot ponies neigh in binary and kids race hover-cows in the arena of chrome.
The Haunted Bubble House fizzes and pops — every ghost is friendly and smells faintly of soda.
Dragon Tacos and Fire Fries await at the Lava Lunch Plaza. It's spicy, silly, and definitely not FDA approved.
Visit Cloud 9 Cinema for a zero-gravity movie experience — popcorn floats, but plot holes still hit hard.
The Time Traveler’s Tea Party only lasts 5 minutes but feels like a full afternoon — don’t blink!
Fluffball Forest has pillow fights on the hour and trampoline paths that bounce you into nearby dreams.
Finally, exit through the Gift-a-saurus Graveyard, where extinct plushies roam and hugs are mandatory.
